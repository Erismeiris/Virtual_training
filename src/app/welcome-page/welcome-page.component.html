Summary of OpenBoard software

OpenBoard is a free, open-source online whiteboard tool that is packed with features. It is a great option for teachers, students, and anyone who needs a collaborative whiteboard tool. Some of the key features of Openboard include:

  • Variety of tools:such as pens, highlighters, erasers, shapes, lines, text, and more. It also includes a number of math tools, such as a protractor, compass, and ruler.
  • Interactive activities: Openboard allows you to create interactive activities for your students. For example, you can create a simple quiz where students can submit their answers.
  • Documents: Openboard allows you to add documents to your whiteboard. This is a great way to keep your whiteboard organized and to share documents with your students.
  • Videos and audio: Openboard allows you to insert videos and audio files into your whiteboard. This is a great way to add multimedia to your lessons.
  • Free and open-source: Openboard is free to use and open-source. This means that you can use it for free and that you can modify the code to fit your needs.

Tutorial guide

  • Download and install OpenBoard You can download Openboard from the Openboard website. Start Openboard. Once Openboard is installed, you can start it by clicking on the Openboard icon.
  • : The toolbar on the left side of the screen contains a variety of tools that you can use to draw, write, and add objects to your whiteboard
  • Use the tools: To use a tool, simply click on it and then click on the whiteboard where you want to use it.
  • Add document: To add a document to your whiteboard, click on the "Documents" button and then select the document you want to add.
