PROPERTIES OF 2D SHAPES Grade 5

Term Topic
Term 3
  • Describe sort and compare 2D shapes in terms of:
    • Straight and curved sides
    • Number and length of sides
    • Angles in shapes, limited to
      1. right angles
      2. Angles smaller than right angles
      3. Angles greater than right angles
  • Draw 2D shapes on grid paper
  • Recognise and describe angles in 2D shapes:
    • Right angles
    • Angles smaller than right angles
    • Angles greater than right angles
