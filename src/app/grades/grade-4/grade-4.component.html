Annual Teaching Plan

PROPERTIES OF 2D SHAPES Grade 4

Term Topic
Term 4
  • Range of shapes: Recognize, visualize and name 2-D shapes in the environment and geometric setting, focusing on:
    • regular polygons
    • irregular polygons
    • triangles
    • squares, rectangles, other quadrilaterals, pentagons, hexagons, heptagons
    • Circles
  • Characteristics of shapes: Describe, sort and compare 2-D shapes in terms of:
    • straight and curved sides
    • number of sides
Further activities: Draw 2-D shapes on grid paper
